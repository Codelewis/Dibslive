Our Mission

Our mission at Dibs is to divert useful items from the trash
for reuse and upcycling by other people, to reduce waste sent to landfills and build more sustainable communities.

Dibs leverages the trust found within communities to facilitate
the exchange of items amongst individuals in a local setting.

This way, Dibs helps maximize the chance that an item can be
fully utilized by other people before it ends up in a landfill.

We hope to empower communities with tools to effectively
chart a path to achieving sustainability and Zero Waste.
